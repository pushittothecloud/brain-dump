⚙️ Work & Commitments
  • Hard deadlines: Report due today, deliverable by Friday
  • Critical issues: Bug/outage affecting users, boss waiting
  • High-consequence promises: Things you told someone with consequences
  • Career: upcoming interview, imminent opportunity
  • ⚠️ Skip for now: Slack messages, generic emails, vague deadlines
🏠 Home & Systems
  • Broken & blocking: No heat, leak, no internet, broken door
  • Safety issues: Anything that could cause harm
  • Last-minute move deadline, time-sensitive repairs
  • ⚠️ Skip for now: Organizing closet, "should clean eventually", vague fixes
👥 People, Events & Waiting
  • Scheduled commitments: Meeting in 2 hours, dinner tonight, call at 3pm
  • Someone waiting NOW: Response they're expecting today
  • Time-specific promises: Deadline you committed to, event you promised
  • Escalating conflicts that need immediate resolution
  • ⚠️ Skip if vague: Generic "call mom", follow-ups without specific dates
💰 Money & Life Admin
  • Hard cutoffs: Bill due TODAY (penalties if late), rent deadline
  • Crisis: Fraud alert, failed payment, eviction notice
  • Taxes near filing deadline, forms with expiration dates
  • Subscriptions & savings: Cancel unused services, review recurring charges, cut unnecessary spending
  • ⚠️ Skip for now: Browsing deals, "organize budget someday", vague planning
🧠 Worries, Avoidance & Mental Load
  • Decision that's blocking: Can't move forward without deciding this
  • Avoidance with consequences: Thing you're avoiding that has a deadline or impact
  • What's actually urgent vs. what FEELS urgent right now
  • Anxiety about time-sensitive things
🔁 Open Loops & Time Pressure
  • Blocking other tasks: Can't move forward on X until this is done
  • Interrupted projects with upcoming deadline
  • Things on your mind that feel unresolved
  • ⚠️ Skip if vague: "Finish eventually", no clear deadline or consequence
🧭 Direction, Growth & Decisions
  • Decision with deadline: "Must decide by end of week"
  • Opportunity with expiration date (apply by Friday)
  • Usually Q2/Q4, but flag if time-sensitive
  • ⚠️ Skip if vague: Dreams without deadlines, vague long-term goals
🏥 Health, Energy & Self
  • Urgent medical: Appointment scheduled, symptoms worsening, time-sensitive meds
  • Health crisis: Injury, severe symptoms, medication needed now
  • ⚠️ Skip vague goals: "Get healthy", "exercise more" without specific appointments
✨ Wants & Desires
  • Ideas, projects, things you want to explore
  • Skills or habits you want to build
  • Things that excite or inspire you
  • Bucket list items, dreams, aspirations
📅 Deadlines & Time-Sensitive
  • Specific dates things are due
  • Events, meetings, appointments
  • Hard cutoffs and time-critical commitments
  • What must be done by when
